Glazed Meatloaf

This is a recipe of delicous Glazed Meatloaf

This will be a perfect family meal, this recipe is mean't to feed 4 but can be doubled to feed more if need be. Please give the recipe a try and let me know how it taste

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, divided
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 slices bread, broken up to small pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon beef bouillon granules

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a small bowl, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice and mustard powder
  3. In a separate large bowl, combine the ground beef, bread, onion, egg, bouillon, remaining lemon juice and 1/3 of the ketchup mixture from the small bowl. Mix this well and place in a 5x9 inch loaf pan
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour, drain any excess fat, coat with remaining ketchup mixture and bake for 10 more minutes

Per Serving: 589 calories; protein 27.9g; carbohydrates 25.2g; fat 41.7g; cholesterol 159.7mg; sodium 499.9mg
